## Cross-Persona Insights

### What's Working:
1. **Proof_lines are gold** - Every persona valued seeing their own words crystallized. These are the "quotable moments" of growth.
2. **Patterns are actionable** - The patterns field ("physical handoff before I can solve it") becomes a tactic they can reuse.
3. **FIRES extraction surfaces truth** - Seeing which elements appear across entries reveals what actually matters to them.
4. **The 48-hour question lands** - When it's specific to their situation, it prompts real reflection.

### What's Missing:
1. **CONNECTION TO PREDICTION** - Nobody sees how their daily entries connect to their stated goal. The loop isn't closed.
2. **SYNTHESIS** - Data is captured but never summarized. "Based on your 5 entries, here's what's emerging..."
3. **PROGRESSION** - No sense of movement. Is my zone changing? Am I getting closer to my goal?
4. **GOAL-SPECIFIC METRICS** - Zone scores feel generic. Each persona wants metrics tied to THEIR goal:
   - Marcus: Trust rebuilt (qualitative)
   - VP: Decision clarity (trending)
   - Frank: Mike's independence (quantifiable)
   - Founder: Hours of freedom (trackable)

### What Currently Shows in Together vs What Matters:

| Feature | Currently Shows | What Personas Actually Want |
|---------|----------------|---------------------------|
| Zone cards | Static placement | Movement/progression over time |
| Predictability score | A number (75, 80, etc) | Context: "Is this good? What changes it?" |
| Story sections | Empty/null | Populated with AI-synthesized narrative |
| Campfire | Community feed | Their OWN wins in a "proof wall" format |
| Predictions | One prediction, no check-in | Loop closure: "Here's what changed since you predicted" |

---

## The Biggest Takeaway

All four personas did the work. They have rich data - meaningful proof_lines, validated patterns, FIRES elements across entries. But the **synthesis layer is missing**. The tool collected evidence but never reflected it back in a way that says:

> "You predicted X. You've logged 5 entries. Here's what your data shows is working, what's shifting, and what your next move might be."

**The data exists. The story isn't being told.**

---

## Recommended Priority Features (Based on All 4 Personas):

1. **"Your Proof Wall"** - Proof_lines and integrity_lines displayed as quotable wins
2. **Pattern Playbook** - The patterns field surfaced as "tactics that work for you"
3. **Prediction Loop Closure** - "Your prediction was X. Here's what 5 entries reveal about progress."
4. **FIRES Frequency** - "Influence appeared in 4/5 entries" as insight
5. **Goal-Specific Progress** - Custom metric tied to their stated goal, not generic zones
